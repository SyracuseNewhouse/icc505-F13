Societal shifts
Terrorism and security changes ever since

Pan Am Flight 103 exploded over Lockerbie, Scotland on Dec. 21, 1988, killing 270 people. Twenty five years later, everything has changed: from laws to airport security to joint investigations to the way the media covers terrorist attacks to the word "terrorism" itself.
